COMMUNITY AFFAIRS POLICY

FAITHCREDA & SONS Community Affairs program is designed to allow a proactive approach to communicate with the host communities. We will foster healthy long-term relationships with communities through the observance of the highest standards in conduct. To accomplish this, we will strive to maintain good public relations with every host community.


We will educate all personnel in proper community affairs awareness. Every new employee will have to attend an introductory course on community affairs so as to be acquainted with community related issues and how to address them. Hence, we consider it a duty for every employee to report any confrontations with host communities, and prevent incidents that lead to confrontations between him/her and the host communities. This is considered to be very key to our operations.

Accurate and timely reporting of all hostile incidents, confrontations or misunderstandings with host communities will give management the opportunity to investigate and eliminate the underlying causes

All sub-contractors are expected to conduct their activities in a way that is completely in accordance with the company’s community affairs policy. Our goal is to foster healthy longterm relationship with communities through the observance of the highest standards in our conduct, and to develop mutually beneficial solution that will reduce or eradicate the impact of our operations on the host community’s environment.

Safety POLICY

It is the policy of FAITHCREDA & SONS to conduct all our activities in a manner that the health and safety of all employees, sub-contractors and the host communities are properly safeguarded. We will conduct our activities/operations in line with the company’s safety guidelines, the requirements of our clients and all relevant Government agencies.

We are dedicated to the precept of maintaining the highest possible safety standards, with the same dedication to production/services, and consider these two to be inseparable. An efficient operation is a safe operation, and no operation can be considered to be efficient if it is not safe. This maxim is the heart of our safety policy

FAITHCREDA & SONS has in place a program of education in proper safety awareness for all personnel. New employees will be made to attend an induction/orientation course on safety matters. We believe that it is the duty of every employee to prevent self-injury and to safeguard against injury to co-workers.

Our personnel will adhere to the procedures for reporting unsafe acts and working practices, LTIs and near misses. Accurate reporting will give management the opportunity to identify and eliminate the causes of LTIs and the near misses, which is the ultimate goal of our safety policy.

All sub-contractors will be made to conduct their activities in a way that is completely in line with the company’s safety policy. Our goal is to work together in improving our awareness of our own safety as well as the safety of all persons involved in our operations and/or to be affected by our activities.

occupational health policy

FAITHCREDA & SONS will protect and promote the health of her workforce as well as conduct her activities in such a manner that will not adversely affect any third party. We will make every conscious effort to provide a work place free from health hazard.

To establish a proactive occupational health program to identify and control any health hazard.

To ensure that every new employee is physically fit to perform the job he is hired to do.

To ensure that every employee undergoes physical examination on regular basis to proactively identify any health hazard that could affect the workforce on the one hand and impair our operations on the other.

To provide facilities capable of responding to emergency situations that relate to occupational health.

To maintain retainer clinics/hospitals that would provide specialized services whenever the need arises.

To provide first aid facilities at all work locations.

To provide emergency evacuation facilities and procedures at all work sites/locations.

To promote sound health hygiene standards and,

To promote sound and adequate health education.

All sub-contractors will be made to conduct their activities in a way that is completely in line with the company’s Occupational Health Policy. Our goal is to work together in improving our Occupational Health Awareness as well as the Occupational Health Awareness of all persons involved in our operations.

Environmental POLICY

It is our policy at FAITHCREDA & SONS to conduct all activities in a manner that the environment in which we operate is properly safeguarded. Our operations are conducted in line with the Company’s Environmental guidelines, standards, the requirements of our clients and all relevant Government agencies.

We are dedicated to the precept of minimizing the effect of our operations on the environment. It is our belief that no operation can remain efficient if it is not environmental friendly, suitable and sustainable.

An excellent educational program is put in place to enlighten all personnel in proper environmental awareness and recycling. Every employee is made to undergo an induction course in which environmental awareness is a prominent element. We consider it the duty of every employee to practice sound environmental management that will emphasize proper disposal of waste materials so as to maintain a clean and safe working environment.

Every Staff of FAITHCREDA & SONS is expected to:

  1. Adhere to the practices for reporting spills and other environmentally unacceptable practices.
  2. Follow proper disposal methods, develop and maintain sound recycling efforts.

Accurate and timely spill reporting and other unfriendly environmental practices will afford management the opportunity to identify and eliminate the causes and effects of environmental spills and pollutions, which is the ultimate goal of our environmental policy.

All sub-contractors are expected to conduct their activities in such a manner that is consistent with the Company’s Environmental Policy. Our goal is to work together in improving the environmental awareness of all persons involved in our operations.
